About Me

I am a second year Computer Science Ph.D. student at the University of Chicago, in the Systems Group. My research focus is in managing power and energy consumption on embedded platforms, which is part of the Self-aware Computing (SEEC) project. My advisor is Hank Hoffmann.

Prior to attending graduate school, I spent five years working in industry as a software engineer.
